AMCET

Annai Mira College of Engineering and Technology (AMCET), Vellore is an Engineering College started in 2012 under the R.T. Educational Trust, Vellore. It is situated in a sprawling campus of 25 acres in the Chennai-Bengaluru National Highways where many of the Government Buildings, Commercial Buildings and Hospitals are located.
